What is CitizenAI?

CitizenAI is a smart assistant that helps people connect more easily with their local government. Whether you're reporting problems like potholes, finding public services, or staying updated on news — CitizenAI makes it all simple and accessible.

CitizenAI can be used by students, residents, and local officials alike. Whether it's finding information about nearby events, requesting community services, or offering civic feedback — the platform adapts to different needs with clarity and ease.

Why Use CitizenAI?

Navigating government systems can be frustrating and time-consuming — but CitizenAI simplifies that. It brings government information and services together in one place, with an easy-to-use AI interface.

It's especially useful for people who may struggle with complicated websites or paperwork. With CitizenAI, everyone gets clear answers, faster responses, and a smoother connection to their community.

Key Features:

  • 🗺️ Easy access to local services and announcements
  • 💬 AI-powered chatbot for fast, reliable answers
  • 📝 Submit feedback or issues with just a few clicks
  • 📊 Personalized dashboards for citizens
  • 🔐 Secure and user-friendly login system
